Razvijmo Taylorjevo vrsto naprej (naprej, zaradi 훾lena $+h$): $$f{\left (x + h \right )} =\sum_{n=0}^{\infty}h^n\frac{d^n}{dx^n}f(x)= f{\left (x \right )} + h \frac{d}{d x} f{\left (x \right )} + \underbrace{h^2\,\frac{d^2}{dx^2}f(x)+\cdots}_{\mathcal{O}\left(h^{2}\right)}$$
